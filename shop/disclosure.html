Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency is a core Blushless value. This page explains how we earn revenue through product links on our website.

How We Earn

Some of the links on blushless.com are affiliate links. This means that when you click through to a retailer and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you.

Which Links Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links appear on our Shop pages and product pages. These include links to retailers such as:

  • Amazon (via Amazon Associates Program)
  • Sephora (via Rakuten Advertising)
  • Ulta Beauty (via CJ Affiliate)
  • Nordstrom (via Rakuten Advertising)
  • Dermstore (via ShareASale)

Does This Affect the Price?

No. You pay the same price whether you use our affiliate links or go directly to the retailer. The commission comes from the retailer, not from you. There is zero additional cost to you.

Does This Affect Our Recommendations?

No. Our product recommendations are based on our expertise in sculpted cosmetics and facial architecture. We only recommend products that meet our standards for quality, performance, and alignment with the Blushless philosophy. Commission rates do not influence which products we feature or which retailers appear first.

FTC Compliance

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) guidelines, we disclose that blushless.com contains affiliate links. We are participants in affiliate programs including the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, Rakuten Advertising, CJ Affiliate, and ShareASale.

Commission Rates

For transparency, typical commission rates from our affiliate partners range from 1% to 15% depending on the retailer and product category. These rates are set by the retailers, not by us.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our affiliate relationships or this disclosure, please contact us. We believe in full transparency about how this website generates revenue.

Last updated: February 2026. This disclosure applies to all pages on blushless.com that contain affiliate links.
